**Share Your Story** is a conversational storytelling app that helps people capture their life stories by talking naturally with Claude. Priceless family stories disappear every day, not because people don't want to share, but because nobody sits down and asks in the right way.

When we designed it, we focused on a few things:

- **Claude is the listener, not the author.** It asks warm, context-aware follow-ups like a friend at the kitchen table. It never writes the story for you. We prompt it to follow tangents and handle sensitive topics gently.
- **Voice-first and multilingual.** People just talk. Real-time transcription and text-to-speech support 32 languages, so a grandmother in Seoul or a dad in Mumbai can share in the language they actually think in.
- **Privacy by default.** Stories stay on the device. Nothing leaves without the user's say-so, and anything can be deleted anytime.
- **Conversations become keepsakes.** Everything compiles into a readable book with chapters that you can flip through and revisit. Then export to an audiobook or shareable reel.

Looking ahead, we want to explore using the app for language preservation in endangered language communities and add more rigorous content sensitivity options for handling difficult topics like trauma or loss.
